My Story

Creative work has always been the through-line.

I’m Chad Blincoe, a Florida-based web designer and developer who enjoys building digital work that feels both polished and personal. I like the mix of structure and creativity that web design demands: the logic of building something correctly, and the artistic judgment of making it feel right.

What keeps me engaged is problem solving. Whether I’m refining a layout, improving user flow, or rebuilding a page so it works better on mobile, I like the process of taking something from rough to refined.

My background includes front-end development, graphic design, and digital marketing. That mix helps me think beyond just code. I’m interested in how a site looks, how it communicates, and how it supports the goal behind the project.
